AboutWITH LOVE

2021

It was a simple, friendly idea. Start a brand and just sell clothes that I found cool.
That's all it was and had been for a while. Then, as time passed on and my mind and identity
grew with me, I realized what I had isn't just a brand but a domain where I can
share my interests to others across the web and world. I always had a fascination with
culture, art, and history and I want to display everything that I can possibly find into
one single space with the goal that it might inspire or educate someone to wanna learn
or discover more about the world.

The clothes are just the merchandise, like when you go to a concert to see your favorite
artist or band perform. You get something that's a piece of them to be with you forever
cause you support them. I didn't want to put a price on everything people can get from
WITH LOVE. Just being here, viewing everything, and even reading this page
means more to me than anything. Thank you so much and I'd love to see how
this website evolves with time.
