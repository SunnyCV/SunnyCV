What I Can Do

My range is wide. I can take a brand from nothing — logo, identity, website, content, the whole thing — and build it into something that feels alive. I work across print, digital, apparel, photography, video, and sound. I research trends, consult on direction, and handle production from concept to final file.

Brand & Identity Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, UI/UX, color theory, trend research, mood board creation
Print & Apparel Large-scale industrial printing, screen printing, apparel graphics, production coordination
Photo & Film DSLR photography, studio lighting, videography, editing & post-production workflows
Sound & Web Audio mixing, sound design, web design & coding, digital & social media content
